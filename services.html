Kitchen

We think of the kitchen as the centerpiece of a house I am sure we are not the only ones who realize that almost every house in Denver was built in the early 1900’s. That being said, it is very common to see kitchens with outdated appliances, cabinets that do not meet your style standards, and kitchens that are closed off from the rest of the house. It is the 21st century. Let Facelift Remodeling modernize your kitchen with your ideas and our precise execution.

We specialize in:

  • Design: Modern, traditional, and rustic are our bread and butter but are open to just about style your heart desires
  • Materials: we only use the best of the best materials for your custom cabinets, countertops, and additional features.
  • The whole package: we start working with you from day 1 - from planning to the execution of your kitchen remodel.
