Kitchen

We think of the kitchen as the centerpiece of a house. I am sure we were not the only ones to recognize that almost every house in Denver was built in the early 1900’s. That being said, it is very common to see kitchens with outdated cabinets, countertops, and appliances that do not meet your style standards. Also let’s address the elephant in the room - closed off kitchens. It may seem like a daunting task, but we have vast experience restructuring kitchens to give your home that warming open floor plan feel. It is the 21st century. Let Facelift Remodeling modernize your kitchen with your ideas and our precise execution.

We specialize in:

  • Design: Modern, traditional, and rustic are our bread and butter but are open to just about style your heart desires
  • Materials: we only use the best of the best materials for your custom cabinets, countertops, and additional features.
  • The whole package: we start working with you from day 1 - from planning to the execution of your kitchen remodel.

Bathroom

Elegant, peaceful, spacious: Do these words come to mind when you think of your bathroom? If not, Facelift Remodeling is here to help! Bathrooms are a small but impactful room in your home. A bathroom should be a sanctuary in your home where you feel at rest. Whether it is completely revamping your master bedroom to a small project on your half bathroom, Facelift Remodeling has the capability to turn your bathroom into a masterpiece.

Our bathroom expertise:

  • Painting: a fresh coat of paint goes a long way to refresh your personal space
  • Redesign: bathrooms are often crowded and cluttered which is why Facelift is here to make this a space where you can feel organized and free
  • Installments: shower, bath, toilet, vanity, you name it, we can do it!
